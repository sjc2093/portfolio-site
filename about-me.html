My journey began in the fast-paced culinary industry, where I excelled as a chef. With a strong foundation in professional cookery, I honed my skills at renowned establishments such as John's House and The Orange Tree, where I mastered the art of preparing dishes to Michelin Star standards. I thrived in high-pressure environments, overseeing various aspects of the culinary process, including ingredient sourcing, menu design, and team management. My attention to detail, creativity, and commitment to excellence allowed me to deliver exceptional dining experiences while mentoring and training fellow chefs.

Seeking new challenges and embracing my passion for technology and creativity, I transitioned into the digital world as a Digital Marketing Apprentice. During this time, I developed a diverse skill set encompassing website development, content creation, social media management, and data analysis. Leveraging tools like WordPress, Google Analytics, and Adobe Creative Suite, I crafted engaging blog posts, optimized user experiences, and enhanced brand presence across various platforms. This experience opened doors to my current role at My Car Import, where I serve as an Import Logistics Co-ordinator. With my meticulous organizational skills and attention to detail, I manage European transport arrangements, handle customs clearance procedures, and ensure seamless deliveries and appointments for clients.

By combining my culinary expertise, digital marketing skills, and logistical proficiency, I bring a unique perspective to my role as a front-end developer. I am passionate about creating visually stunning and highly functional websites that captivate users and deliver exceptional online experiences. Let's collaborate to transform your vision into a reality, leveraging my diverse background and technical expertise to bring your web projects to life.
